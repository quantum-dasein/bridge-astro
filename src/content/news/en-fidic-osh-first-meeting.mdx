The first **FIDIC OSH** meeting was held. Participants discussed not only the application of FIDIC contracts, but also the practical challenges faced by infrastructure professionals:

- contract management and the role of the Engineer;
- construction quality standards;
- regulatory gaps in process management;
- lack of professional knowledge exchange.

One key outcome of the meeting was the initiative to establish **FIDIC Club** — a professional community for specialists in the field. We thank all participants for the substantive and open dialogue.

> Thank you to everyone who joined for the meaningful discussion.
