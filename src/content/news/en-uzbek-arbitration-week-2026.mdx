On **18 June 2026**, Tashkent will host the international conference **“Shaping the New Legal Landscape of Central Asia: Arbitration, Investment and Market Development”**, included in the official programme of **Uzbek Arbitration Week** and the **Tashkent International Investment Forum**.

The event is organised by **Chervets.Partners**, the **Tashkent International Arbitration Centre (TIAC)** and **MGIMO Tashkent**. Bridge Consult takes part in the conference as a specialist in FIDIC infrastructure contracts and dispute resolution.

## Who Will Gather Around the Table

The conference will bring together members of the international arbitration community, investors, heads of legal functions, law firm partners, academics and representatives of state institutions from **Central Asia, the Middle East and Russia**.

## Key Topics

Delegates will discuss the issues that directly shape the investment appeal of states and projects:

- the development of Uzbekistan's legal market;
- modern dispute resolution mechanisms;
- the transformation of international arbitration;
- legal instruments for improving investment attractiveness.

The event will also feature a joint analytical project by Chervets.Partners and Birge, dedicated to Uzbekistan's investment environment and the prospects for further development of the country's business climate.

The sessions will be moderated by **Evgenia Chervets**, Managing Partner of Chervets.Partners, and **Maria Lyubimova**, Partner of the firm.

> **18 June 2026 · MGIMO Tashkent, Tashkent**
