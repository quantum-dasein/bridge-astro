01 · About the programme

A single system,
not a set of courses

Most programmes address FIDIC contracts as separate disciplines. In practice, contract administration, claims, delay analysis and dispute resolution are interconnected and work as a single project-management system.

Summer School is not an introductory course. It is designed for professionals with hands-on experience applying FIDIC contracts.

The full lifecycle of a contractual issue

From risk allocation and the occurrence of an event — through notices, claims, analysis of impact on time and cost, Engineer's determinations and possible dispute resolution.

A working-session format, not a lecture

Participants analyse real contractual situations from the perspective of every project party — discussing the consequences of decisions, approaches to risk, claim preparation and dispute avoidance.

Employer Engineer Contractor Contract Manager Dispute Consultant

Real cases from international projects

Built on projects financed by international financial institutions and the practical experience of the experts. Covers the Red, Yellow, Silver and White Books (1999 and 2017 editions).

The programme follows a “from practice to theory” format: participants work in groups on concrete practical assignments using the theoretical materials of the FIDIC courses.
